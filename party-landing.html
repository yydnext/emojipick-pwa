EmojiPick Party Mode
A 1-minute party game you can start with a link. No sign-up.
How it works
  1. Enter your name → Create Room
  2. Tap Copy / Share (or show QR)
  3. Friends open the link → Enter name → Join
Works on mobile & desktop. Rooms are temporary. Email is optional (only for early access updates).
