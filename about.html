EmojiPick
Pick emojis → Get lucky numbers → Share & challenge

About

EmojiPick is a fun game simulation. It does not use real lottery results and does not support real-money wagering. You pick emojis (or numbers in Party Mode), the app generates simulated “winning numbers,” and you compare results for entertainment.

Reminder: This app is for fun only. Always play responsibly if you buy real lottery tickets.

How to Play

Solo (Main screen)

  • Select a game (Powerball / Mega Millions / TX Instant demo).
  • Pick the required emojis.
  • The app generates lucky numbers for entertainment.
  • Use Compare to see differences, and save/share your picks.

Party Mode (Same device)

  • Open Party Mode and choose a game and number of players.
  • Enter player names (optional).
  • (Optional) Add a Winning seed so the group “creates” the winning numbers together.
  • Tap Start Party. Each player takes a turn selecting numbers, then taps Confirm → Lock in.
  • After all rounds finish, check the Leaderboard for winners, round results, and each player’s pick.

Scoring example: main matches × 10 + bonus match × 5 (per round).
