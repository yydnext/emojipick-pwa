What this is

EmojiPick is a lightweight, just-for-fun tool that turns your emoji choices into a lottery-style number set. It does not predict outcomes and is not affiliated with any lottery.

Key features

  • Generate a number set from 6 emojis
  • Save picks on this device (localStorage)
  • Share a link to your pick page
  • Compare against winning numbers (manual input)
